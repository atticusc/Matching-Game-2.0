Matching Game Instructions

Start screen
1. Create the board
2. Set each card on the board, face down
3. If the card clcked, flip over
4. Click a second card, flip it over
5. If the faces of both cards match remove the cards. If the faces do not 
match flip them back over
6. Repeat from step 3 untill all cards are removed
7. End game play again
